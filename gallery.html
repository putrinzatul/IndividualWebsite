Happiness is the feeling of joy, contentment, or satisfaction that people experience. It can come from positive experiences, relationships with others, or achieving personal goals. People feel happy when they enjoy their lives, feel loved by others, or do things they care about. Happiness is different for everyone what makes one person happy might not work for someone else. It can be a moment of fun with friends or a lasting sense of peace from meaningful relationships, and it’s influenced by both emotions and actions like being grateful or taking care of oneself.
